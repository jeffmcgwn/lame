About us

Lame Genie is the premier video game music cover band (heavy rock/metal) in Providence, RI.

Formed in 2013, Lame Genie has made a name for themselves through their polished, heavy video game covers and memorable live shows with sync'd visuals and projections. Having recorded over 14 albums in their 7 year span, this is a band that doesn't plan on stopping anytime soon.

They have been featured at many notable conventions, namely:

Super MagFest (MD) (2019 / 2020)
PAX East (MA) (2016 / 2017 / 2018 / 2019)
Too Many Games (PA) (2019)
Bit Gen Festival (MD) (2018 / 2020)
Retro Games Con (NY) (2018 / 2019)
ReplayFX (PA) (2019)
NEON Festival (RI) (2018 / 2020)
CTGamerCon (CT) (2016 / 2019)
Retro World Expo (CT) (2016 / 2019)
+ many more.

Their live shows are an experience you won't soon forget
